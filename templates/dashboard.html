❤️ Heart Disease

Heart disease refers to several types of heart conditions, including coronary artery disease and heart attacks.

  • Symptoms: Chest pain, shortness of breath, fatigue
  • Risk Factors: Smoking, high blood pressure, high cholesterol, obesity
  • Prevention: Exercise regularly, eat healthy, manage stress, get regular checkups

🩺 Cardiovascular disease is the leading cause of death globally.

💉 Diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic condition that affects how your body turns food into energy due to insulin issues.

  • Types: Type 1, Type 2, Gestational
  • Symptoms: Excessive thirst, frequent urination, fatigue, blurry vision
  • Prevention: Maintain a healthy weight, balanced diet, exercise, avoid smoking

📊 1 in 10 adults globally is living with diabetes.
